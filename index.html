Gianluca Filippone

Postdoctoral researcher @ Gran Sasso Science Institute, L'Aquila, Italy

Hi, I am Gianluca and I am a postdoctoral researcher at the Gran Sasso Science Institute (GSSI), in L'Aquila, Italy.
I received my PhD in Information and Communication Technology at the University of L'Aquila in 2023. Previously, I obtained my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science in 2016 and my Master's degree in Computer Science in 2019 at the University of L'Aquila.
My main research interests are on Software Engineering for adaptive systems, focusing in particular on the model-driven composition, coordination, and adaptation of distributed systems, multi-robot systems, and microservice architectures.
